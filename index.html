Our Story

The Lipstick Pig BBQ started as a humble backyard passion project. Our founder, a dedicated BBQ enthusiast, spent years perfecting family recipes over a slow-burning fire. The name "Lipstick Pig" comes from a playful tradition: a kiss of our signature tangy sauce on every perfect piece of meat.

We believe that great BBQ is more than just food; it's a labor of love, patience, and community. We use only the finest ingredients, hand-rubbing each cut of meat with our secret blend of spices before smoking it for hours to achieve that tender, melt-in-your-mouth texture.
